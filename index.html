home

Introducing eat Deli & Bar. Great food, great coffee, great wines, great service, great prices notice the trend?

We believe we are the ideal stopover for breakfast, brunch, lunch, coffee or a light snack, depending on your schedule or how long you wish to stay!

Bottom line is, if youre traveling up or down the South Island of New Zealand, between Christchurch and Queenstown, eat Deli & Bar is the place to stop.

The moment you walk in you realise our place is special. Relax and unwind while enjoying the urban feel we've created - alfresco in summer or tucked up on the deep buttoned leather lounge by the fire in winter.

Located at the gateway of the Mackenzie Country and magnificent Aoraki Mount Cook, eat offers the best in fast, deli style food, easy on the sedentary stomach and the wallet! 

While stopping with us take the opportunity to use our internet connection - complimentary to all our patrons.

We hope that this site gives you a little understanding of what we offer, how we do it and also why we do it.
