76 Main Street, Fairlie
South Canterbury
New Zealand

Fairlie township is nestled in the Fairlie Basin at the southern end of the Mackenzie district, just 2 hours drive from Christchurch.

The township is at the intersection of S.H 79 ( Fairlie - Geraldine) and S.H 8 ( Timaru - Omarama), the half way point on the Christchurch / Queenstown tourist route.

Eat Deli & Bar is located on the corner of the main intersection in the township. We are easy to find and in the unlikely event of you getting lost - just ask one of the locals!
